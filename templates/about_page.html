Peleus Forecasting: The World Structured

Welcome to Peleus Forecasting! We at Peleus are committed to improving the quality of political and social information and the mechanisms through which we record and share it.

We believe that the world is a better, safer and more productive place the more we know about each other, our opinions and the way we think. Everyone – from the amateur news consumer to professional investors and corporations to legislative policy staffs – benefits from a more organized way of recording and presenting our political and social predictions and analysis.

To that end, our open-source forecasting and analysis platform is designed to address inefficiencies in the deployment of crowd-sourced forecasting and academic analysis due to ranking, organization and sorting deficiencies. The platform is designed to serve as

  • A public source of expert level political predictions and analysis
  • Another platform of exposure for professors, think tank fellows,
  • A marketplace for user-to-user identification, through which users can journalists and students to highlight their work and publications. collaborate to build cooperative analysis exchange networks and professional consumers can engage users and user groups for forecasting and/or analysis services.
